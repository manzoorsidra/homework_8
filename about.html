A little bit about me...

A UX Designer at heart, I enjoy spending my time learning about design and people. I strive to create solutions that solve real issues through a human-centered approach.

In 2019, I recieved my Bachelor’s in Interactive Media and Business from New York University Shanghai where I learned to tell stories through technology and design experiences that produce delight. Currently, i’m pursuing a Master of Human - Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon University.

What I've Learned About Design

Users are complicated.
Take time to understand them.

There is no “one-size-fits all” principle.
Design is very context-dependent.

It’s okay to not have all the answers.
Trust the process and trust your team.

Mistakes are a part of the process.
It’s not about getting it right the first time.
